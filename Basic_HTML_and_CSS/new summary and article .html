Champion

Lebron's longevity is unlike any basketball player we've ever seen. His stats speak for themselves. I'd advise you to do a google search on Lebron and see for yourself.

Role Model

He has spoken out about social issues, build schools and businesses, and has become a great man, father, husband etc. The things we can say about Lebron's off the court resume is insane and there's not one negative thing on his resume.
